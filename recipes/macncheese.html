Macaroni and Cheese

    Ingredients

  • Cheese
  • Lobster
  • Collard Greens
  • Noodles

    Steps

  1. Melt the cheese
  2. Mix the noodles with the cheese
  3. Boil the lobster
  4. Mix the lobster meat with macncheese
  5. Cook collard greens and make it a side
