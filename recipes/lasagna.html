Cheap and Easy Recipe!

This awesome lasagna is not only delicious but only 5 dollars. The food is so delicious that even lactose intolerance people will be able to eat this cheese without being hurt.

Lasagna


Ingredients

  • 50ml of cheese
  • 2 scoops of tomato sauce
  • Lean beef

Steps

  1. Pour 300ml of milk into the bucket
  2. Freeze the milk overnight
  3. Mix the frozen milk with cheese until thick consistency
  4. Mix the tomato sauce with the concoction
  5. Fry the beaf with the liquid
  6. Yay we are done
